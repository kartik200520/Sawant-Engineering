Sawant Engineering – Our Journey

About Us

Sawant Engineering Works was founded in 1998 by Raghunath Tukaram Sawant with a vision to deliver world-class precision engineering solutions. Today, we are a trusted partner for industries across Maharashtra, specializing in structural fabrication, sheet metal work, maintenance, jig fixture design, SPM, and automation.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Satara spans 15,000 sq. ft. and houses CNC turning centers, VMC machines, welding robots, and a fully equipped quality lab.

ISO 9001:2015 MSME Certified Make in India Best Employer 2022

Vision

To be the most preferred engineering partner in Western India, known for innovation, quality, and integrity.

Mission

To consistently exceed customer expectations through continuous improvement, employee empowerment, and sustainable practices.

Our Journey

1998

Founded as a small fabrication unit in Satara with 5 employees.

2005

Expanded into CNC machining and jig fixture design.

2012

ISO 9001 certification and new facility inauguration.

2018

Launched automation division (SPM, robotics).

2024

Recognized as one of the top 10 engineering firms in Pune region.
